My Best Movies According to Yemi

My Top 5 Movies of All Time.

The Joker (2019)

The deeper meaning behind Joker and the role society plays in ones mental health is at the forefront in this movie.

The Batman (2022)

This was batman on the screen like we have never seen him before. Understanding that his purpose was bigger than himself.

The Wedding Ringer (2015)

Kevin Hart's funniest movie in my humble opinion.

Forrest Gump (1994)

Resilience and Sympathy were the words that came to my mind. A man who just wants to reunite with his childhood sweetheart.

Life (1999)

To be black men in America once upon a now. Eddie Murphy and Martin Lawrence applied a light hearted spin on it.
